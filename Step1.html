Step 1 : Open the command prompt first.

At the Beginning, we should open the command prompt first. Then we change the directory to your react native folder. Hit expo start. Expo will create a local server on your PC and generate an address through QR code. Scan it with your device which had installed Expo App. Your mobile would open your app inside the Expo App. Fun?
